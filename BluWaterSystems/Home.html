THE BLU STANDARD

OUR GOAL

Throughout our relation you will be presented with most efficient cutting edge technologies that complies with all international, federal, state and local regulations for benefit of reuse to safeguard environmental health. Dedicated to maintain repeatable clean business operation that is test proven.

Provide Solutions that protect human health with costeffective means for treatment of residential, commercial, and industrial water supply and wastewater. We are committed in maintaining professional & courteous level of service to allow continual fulfillment of your water needs.

VISION

TO Be industry leader in the field of the environmental engineering and construction of Water, Wastewater Treatment, Water and Wastewater Networks.


TO Create the highest possible lifecycle value.


TO Serve entire Middle East and African Regions as one stop shop for all your water needs attaining the best possible solutions for our clients via a multi-disciplinary process, based on solid engineering know-how combined with expertise in science, finance, policy and management.

KNOW-HOW



Blu Water has helped clients through all the project phases. Concrete knowledge about EPC of pipe networks, pumping stations, structures, basins and other technical systems.


In the design phase, operations staff are detail sensitive to interaction between hydraulics, sewer system, WWTP and long-term requirements.


Blu Water is specialized in supplying components for over a decade within the wastewater and sludge treatment.


We are continuously developing new Wastewater management solutions. Our ambition is to offer consultancy & contracting services which focus on the development of IT-based tools as a framework for the consultancy services and a full study of the presented case. Whether we are collaborating on a wastewater rehabilitation, operations planning, sewer replacement or sewage discharge into the open country no job is too complex.
